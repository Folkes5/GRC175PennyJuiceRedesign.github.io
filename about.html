We are a family-owned business that was founded in 2001. We have over 40 years of experience in the beverage industry. Our natural fruit juice concentrates are packed with flavor kids love and the nutrition parents and caregivers trust. Simple to mix, colorful, and cost effective.

Penny Juice of America provides natural fruit juice concentrates to childcare centers, promoting healthy hydration with simple, nutritious, and affordable options for kids. Every day, our goal is simple: make it easy for kids to enjoy tasty, nutritious drinks while giving caregivers one less thing to worry about.

Our team at Penny Juice of America is dedicated to delivering nutritious, great-tasting juice and outstanding service to support healthy, happy children. Penny Juice keeps children refreshed and ready to learn every day. From creating delicious, natural juices to making sure every order reaches childcare centers on time, we work together with care, energy, and a big smile.
